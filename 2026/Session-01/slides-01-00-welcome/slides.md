## Evacuation

<Announcement type="info">
  Unlikely to occur
</Announcement>

- **Do not** pack anything up
- Take **only** valuables (e.g. phone, laptop)
- Use **nearest** exit (unless blocked)
- Lecturer **is last to leave**
- Gather as **a group**
- Lecturer will verify everyone exited
- Wait for **all clear** to re-enter

::right:: 

## Lock down

<Announcement type="info">
  Unlikely to occur
</Announcement>

- Draw blinds
- Turn off lights
- Shut door
- Phone on **SILENT** / **Do not** answer
- **NO TALKING**
- **DO NOT** answer door
- Out of sight
